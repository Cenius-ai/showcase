# What lives in this org

Every repository under **Cenius-ai** (other than `.github` and this index) is a
complete application generated end-to-end on [cenius.ai](https://cenius.ai) —
code, design and seeded demo data — published under the MIT license with a
full presentation: README, demo GIF, walkthrough video and screenshots.

## Curation, not a dump

Repos land here on a steady cadence and only after passing curation:

- a real multi-screen recorded preview,
- a clean security scan,
- a distinct design (no near-duplicates),
- a complete, runnable delivery.

## Remix rights

Everything here is MIT. If you modify an app **through cenius.ai** (open it on
the [marketplace](https://cenius.ai/marketplace), prompt for changes), you get
**full rebrand and relicense rights over your derivative**.

## Contributing

These repos are generated artifacts — issues and forks are welcome on the
individual repositories; the catalog itself is maintained by the cenius.ai
publisher pipeline. To get *your* idea built, [start on cenius.ai](https://cenius.ai).
